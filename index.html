Features

Everything you need

Powerful features that make downloading media effortless and enjoyable.

Smart Organize

Automatically creates folders for each channel. Keeps your library pristine and organized without lifting a finger.

SponsorBlock

Integrated API to skip in-video sponsors, intros, and outros automatically. Save hours of your life.

Audio Extraction

Convert videos to crystal clear MP3, FLAC, or WAV. Supports embedding metadata and album art.

Playlist Control

Download full playlists or select specific items (e.g., 1, 5-10). Total control over bulk downloads.

Time Trimming

Don't need the whole video? Cut specific parts by timestamp before downloading.

Modern Dark UI

Built with a sleek, eye-friendly dark mode by default. Responsive and beautiful on Windows.
